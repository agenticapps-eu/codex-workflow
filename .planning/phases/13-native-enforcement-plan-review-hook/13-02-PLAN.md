<objective>
Author the HOOK-02 wrapper script that adapts codex-cli's PreToolUse hook
surface to the unchanged `check-plan-review.sh` gate, and mutation-prove its
fail-CLOSED property (SC#3).

Purpose: `hooks.json` `command` is a static string with no per-invocation
templating, so the file-path the gate needs arrives on stdin JSON. The wrapper
is a thin adapter — it derives the value, execs the gate, and translates the
gate's `{0,2}` exit contract into the hook's `permissionDecision:"deny"` shape
(primary) with a guaranteed-non-empty-stderr `exit 2` fallback. Gate verdict
logic stays entirely in `check-plan-review.sh` (unchanged). The fallback's
empty-stderr-fails-open case is the milestone's nemesis and gets a dedicated
RED→GREEN mutation test.

Output: `skills/agentic-apps-workflow/scripts/hook-wrapper-plan-review.sh`
(shipped automatically by install.sh, which symlinks the whole
`skills/agentic-apps-workflow/` tree) + `test_hook_wrapper_stderr_contract` in
`migrations/run-tests.sh`.
</objective>
