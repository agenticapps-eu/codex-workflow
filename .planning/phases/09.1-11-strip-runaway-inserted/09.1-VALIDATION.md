## Mutation Gate (non-negotiable — criteria 4, 5, 6)

Each named guard MUST be deleted, the suite observed **RED**, and the guard restored. The
recorded RED is the deliverable, not the claim.

**Criterion 5 exists precisely because a green suite survived deleting the guard.** A plan that
asserts "the assertion is live" without recording the RED has *not* satisfied the criterion —
that is the exact failure this phase closes.

**Verify the mutation landed before trusting the result.** In Phase 9 a `sed: bad flag` silently
no-op'd and the run *looked* like a passing demo. An unverified mutation demo is itself a dead
assertion.

## Known Risks to This Strategy

- **Q3 — inverse no-weakening trap.** The un-latch/refuse rule may add a third copy of the anchor
  alternation and break a currently-green `anchor-parity` count assertion. A *good* change failing
  an assertion is not license to weaken it: update the count deliberately and record why.
- **A2 — awk portability.** The composite fix was executed on BSD awk 20200816 only; gawk/mawk are
  not installed. Behavior HIGH confidence, portability MEDIUM.
- **Research freshness.** Valid until ~2026-07-22 — upstream revised 0029 four times in one
  afternoon and merged PR #89 the same day. Re-check `origin/main` before locking D-48's new pin.
